A SOCIAL ART PROJECT

In the social art project “The Monument of Stitches”, knit installation artist Isabel Berglund demonstrated her unique ability to make works of art with hundreds of participants knitting according to a concept she developed. The result was a series of six monumental artworks later assembled at the Trapholt museum in 2016.
